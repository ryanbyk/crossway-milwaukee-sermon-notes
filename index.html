About

CrossWay Milwaukee is a church rooted in Scripture and committed to helping ordinary people follow Jesus together. These notes are designed to help small groups go deeper into the Sunday sermon — not a transcript, but a guide for discussion, reflection, and application.

Each set of notes includes context, a passage summary, the sermon's main points, a key idea, and discussion questions for your group.
